Applied Aerodynamic Research

Simple. Pragmatic. Engineering.

We are a research lab dedicated to solving aerodynamic challenges in transport and energy sectors. Our goal is to allow access and knowledge to the latest technologies in design, analysis, and optimization for aircraft, wind turbines, propellers and other vehicles.
